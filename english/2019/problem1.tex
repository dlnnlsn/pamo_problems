\begin{document}
    Let $(a_n)_{n=0}^{\infty}$ be a sequence of real numbers defined as follows:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item $a_0 = 3$, $a_1 = 2$, and $a_2 = 12$; and
        \item $2a_{n + 3} - a_{n + 2} - 8a_{n + 1} + 4a_n = 0$ for $n \geq 0$.
    \end{itemize}
    Show that $a_n$ is always a strictly positive integer.
