\begin{document}
    Let $x$, $y$, and $z$ be real numbers such that $x < y < z < 6$. Solve the system of inequalities
    \[
        \left\lbrace \begin{array}{c}
           \dfrac{1}{y - x} + \dfrac{1}{z - y} \leq 2 \\
           \dfrac{1}{6 - z} + 2 \leq x. 
        \end{array} \right.
    \]
